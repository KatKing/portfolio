Charleston Donuts

South Carolina's custom order Donut Shop!

Visit the full site.

Just your Average Donut Shop except......

Charleston Donuts focuses on making donuts fresh to order. That means your donut is always hot even at noon! Charleston Donuts has also focused on word of mouth and face to face. They have been considering an online ordering system, but wanted to first launch an easy to use informational website so that their customers and travelors could easily find them. The main focus was taking the existing content and placing in neatly and simply on the website.

The Process

When conducting the interview with the company it was clear that they had a trusted brand name and it was their customer service that made the business thrive. The purpose of this project would make them easier to find and improve their web presence so that they could maintain that great service in a digital fashion. When taking the content from the template it was clear that there was excellent material to work with. A logo that was already known, a clear menu, and some great images.

Objectives

  1. Make this site responsive
  2. Prototype an online odering system for Phase 2.
  3. Make the navigation as simple as possible. No Hamburger Menu!
  4. Let everyone know our Donuts are always hot!

When designing the mobile and tablet views without a hamburger menu, I reviewed was was absolutely needed and what wasn't. Then started with a mobile first so that mobile users wouldn't lose content.

Prototyping the Mobile Navigation

Here was the first mobile wireframe. The main idea was to test some ways of using the navigation. As seen the there is the top navigation and some buttons on the screen. We ultimately went with creating a smaller logo to make a mobile navigation that would be small but easy to read and touch and always visible.
