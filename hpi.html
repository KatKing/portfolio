HPI is the parent of several busineses.

HPI-Online® also known as Hodge Products Inc. is a leading manufacturer and distributor of many products and supplies. They also do business with many leading companies such as Master Lock. The goal for this site was to create a modern and simple design that would serve as a gateway to viewing the products and services under the HPI umbrella, as well as the business partners. There was also a need to provide a way for existing customers to easily access resources and forms.

The Process

This project involved research about what the site does, and who the users are. It was discovered that the majority of users are either looking for Hodge Product sites or existing customers who are re-ordering.

Steps

  1. Layout all items in the simplest way possible.
  2. Create a news feature for interesting industry content.
  3. Customer resources that can be easily shared by service reps.
  4. Make applications and form easy to submit and faxing/printing optional.

The primary ground work for this site was laying it all out in as few pages as possible while keeping it responsive. The design here was primarily in browser prototypes that were tested by the president, the sales team, and some existing users.

The Final Product

Sister Sites in Sortable Easy to read Boxes.
